Maths Warriors was created by Henry Ong, Founder of International Scholastic Trust and SIMCC in 2004 to help students improve in mental sums.

It has been used to train over 100,000 students around the world to compute faster and think strategically to outwit their opponents. Maths Warriors is used in the Singapore International Math Olympiad Challenge (SIMOC) and Singapore National Primary Schools Maths Warriors Challenge since 2004. It will soon be launched in Cambodia and other countries as a national math competition.

Maths Warriors game consists of 2 sets of 6 dice (4, 6, 8, 10, 12 & 20 sided) for 2 players/teams to challenge each other to capture all their opponents dice within the best of 3 rounds to emerge as the winner.
